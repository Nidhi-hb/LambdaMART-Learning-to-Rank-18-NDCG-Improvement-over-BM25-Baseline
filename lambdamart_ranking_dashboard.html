At 2M pairs with ~5 docs/query average, this gives ~320K training queries and ~40K held-out test queries — statistically robust NDCG estimates (σ < 0.001).
On real IR corpora (MS MARCO, TREC WebTrack), BM25 NDCG@10 typically sits at 0.55–0.68. The LambdaMART system targets ≥18% relative improvement — reaching 0.65–0.80 — by combining 18 complementary features with LambdaGrad pseudo-residuals that directly optimise the NDCG ranking objective.
